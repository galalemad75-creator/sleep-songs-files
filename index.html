🌟 About Us

🌙✨

Welcome to Sleep Songs, the go-to destination for gentle, soothing sleep songs that help little ones drift off to dreamland.

🎯 Our Mission

We create a safe, calming audio experience for children ages 4–6, featuring a curated collection of lullabies and soft melodies designed to make bedtime peaceful and happy.

💎 What Makes Us Special

  • 🎵 30 chapters of diverse sleep songs
  • 👶 Kid-friendly content
  • 🛡️ A safe environment — no intrusive ads
  • 📱 Simple, child-friendly interface

👶 Age Group

Our site is designed specifically for children ages 4 to 6, with a bright, engaging visual interface that's easy for young kids to navigate.
